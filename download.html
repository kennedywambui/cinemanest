Download for Android

How to Install

  1. Tap the download button above and wait for the file to finish downloading.
  2. Open the downloaded file (CinemaNest.apk) from your notifications or downloads folder.
  3. If prompted, tap Settings and allow installs from this source — this only needs to be done once.
  4. Tap Install, then open Cinema Nest once it's done.

Cinema Nest isn't listed on the Google Play Store, so Android will show a security notice when installing — this is normal for apps installed outside the Play Store. You can tap "Scan app" to have Google check it, or install directly if you trust the source.

Add to Home Screen

No App Store needed · Works right from Safari

How to Install

  1. Open cinemanest.2bd.net in Safari (this must be Safari — Chrome and other browsers on iOS don't support this).
  2. Tap the Share icon at the bottom of the screen (the square with an arrow pointing up).
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add in the top right corner.
  5. Find the Cinema Nest icon on your home screen and tap it to open — it'll launch fullscreen, just like a regular app.

Apple doesn't allow apps to be installed outside the App Store, so this "Add to Home Screen" method is the standard way to get an app-like experience on iPhone and iPad without going through App Store review.
